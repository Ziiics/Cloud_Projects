# POUR.

### Ideas

POUR will be a free web-based journaling platform that uses guided journaling and AI-asissted prompt to help you understand better of your emotions. This privacy-first journal can be the template when you are dealing wiht overwhelming emtions and need a safe space ot express yourself.

Often, there are times we want to express our feelings to the people we trust the most, family, best friends, and loved ones. However the feeling where you might be judged and be pushed down scares you and you now prefer to keep a "mask" that hold the "you" they expected instead of you yourself. This journal will help you communicate this feelings if you want.

Benefit of journaling:
- Boost emotional intelligence, encourgaes self confidence.
- Inspiring creativity and enhances critical thinking skills
- Learning to open up, disclosing emotions that is harmful for our health, the freedom to express

Improves mood by:
- Tracking any symptoms day-to-day so that you can recognize triggers and learn ways to better control them
- Helps us accept our feelings to have a better psychological health and postiive therapeutic outcomes.

What is journaling?
Journal is a place to safely "sump: what's inside your heads. It helps us better understand adn become more aware of what is going on behind the surface. 

Guidance to write journal
- Write whatever feels right
- Do it somewhere quiet and peaceful
- Create and express what you want from life and how you feel. There are no rules, and there is no wrong way of doing this.
- Do not worry about spelling and punctuation – no one is here to judge you.


Some prompts,
- How do the changes in your life make you feel? And how are you handling changes at work, at home, and in relationships?
- What are you most anxious or uncertain about? Where is that coming from, and how are you coping?
- What three things are you most grateful for today, or what three good things have happened to you today?
- What are your favorite memories from your own or your children’s lives?
- Name something you fear and why?
- What do you enjoy doing and why?
- How would you describe yourself from the perspective of someone close to you?
- What would your very best day look like and why?
- How would a difficult situation be handled if you were being your very best self?
- If you woke up tomorrow having everything you truly wanted, what would it look like?
