Deal with any overwhelming emotion and find a healthy way to express yourself.

Can help:
- Manage anxiety
- Reduce stress
- Cope with depression

Helps improve mood by
- Helping you prioritize problems, fears, and concerns
- Tracking any symptoms day-to-day so that you can recognize triggers and learn ways to better control them
- Providing an opportunity for positive self-talk and identifying negative thoughts and behaviors

How to journal:
- Try to write daily
- Write whatever feels right

Studies shows that writing about stressful and traumatic events can significantly benefit our physical and emotional health.
